Assessment Tools

MBTI (Myers-Briggs Type Indicator)

As a certified MBTI practitioner, I use the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator to help individuals and teams understand their natural preferences in how they perceive the world and make decisions. This powerful tool reveals communication styles, work preferences, and interpersonal dynamics, creating a common language for understanding differences and leveraging diverse strengths within teams.

Format: Individual typing sessions with team sharing and application workshops

Enneagram

As an iEQ9 Accredited Enneagram Practitioner, I guide individuals and teams through the Enneagram system—a profound framework for understanding core motivations, behavioral patterns, and growth paths. Unlike other assessments, the Enneagram reveals the "why" behind behavior, offering deep insight into personal drivers, blind spots, and pathways to development.

Format: Type identification sessions with individual debriefs and integration work
